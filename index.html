Welcome to the Brazil-Macedonia Chamber of Commerce

We promote the strengthening of commercial and cultural relations between Brazil and Macedonia, connecting companies, investors, and professionals for international business opportunities.

About Us

The Brazil-Macedonia Chamber of Commerce is an organization that seeks to facilitate business and cultural exchanges between the two countries. Our goal is to support Brazilian and Macedonian companies in their international operations and create a sustainable cooperation environment.

Services

Business Consulting

We provide guidance for companies wishing to expand their business between Brazil and Macedonia.

Events and Networking

We organize fairs, seminars, and business meetings to strengthen the network of contacts.

Cultural Exchange

We promote cultural activities that encourage integration between the countries.
